# SUS-MAT NRT TRAINING DATA - SKELETON VERSION

cold sintering notes - jordan's project

energy draw: ~2.8 kWh per sintering run (estimated from press spec sheet)
the carver press runs about 3 hours total if you count warmup
instrument: Carver press, model 3851

solvent: DMF, approximately 15 mL per batch for cleaning mold surfaces
also used 10 wt% DI water as transient liquid during sintering (this is separate from the DMF)

ambient conditions: 22°C, 45% RH (thermostat set, not measured)
sintering was run late afternoon most days

PP particles: cryomilled, 250-500 um, sieved with 35/60 mesh
CaSO4: sigma-aldrich 325 mesh, used as-received

got 5-6 dogbones per batch usually, some broke on demolding
the ones from feb 5 batch were pressed late and lab was closing - might have rushed cooldown

waste: DMF solvent residue goes to hazardous waste (EHS pickup tuesdays)
CAS number for DMF is 68-12-2
also some CaSO4 dust - swept up, regular trash

po-hao ran the CT scans over ~2 weeks in march, I don't remember exact dates
he said the voxel resolution was 7.5 um

talked to prof vogt about publishing - he wants us to put data on OSF before submitting
